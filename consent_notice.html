Audio Recording & AI Analysis Notice

Required under Washington State two-party consent law (RCW 9.73.030)
This workspace uses AXIS, an ambient audio recording and AI analysis system. By remaining in this area, you acknowledge that conversations may be recorded and processed.

What is recorded

  • Ambient audio from microphones in the workspace
  • Conversations are transcribed using local speech-to-text software
  • Transcripts are analyzed by AI to produce structured meeting notes

What is NOT recorded

  • Video or images — audio only
  • Personal devices, phone calls, or communications outside this workspace
  • Content is never used for employee surveillance or performance evaluation

How data is handled

  • Audio is processed locally and is not stored after transcription
  • AI-generated summaries are the only output retained
  • Summaries are shared with the team — there are no hidden records
  • All output includes a disclaimer: "AI-generated summary. Not a verbatim transcript."

Your rights

  • You may request that recording be paused during sensitive conversations
  • You may ask to see any session notes that include your contributions
  • If you do not consent, please notify your team lead before the session begins

Contact

Questions about this system can be directed to the team administrator.
